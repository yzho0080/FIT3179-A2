WORLD DATA by country (2020)

This page can help you understand some of the world indexes that are closely connected with our lives by visualizing it.

Demographic change choropleth map

The graph shows the population changes of each country in the world in 2020. A blue country means that the country’s population has decreased over the previous year; a red country means that the country’s population has increased over the previous year. The darker the color means the higher the rate of population change, Australia's population growth is 1.6%.

The country with the longest life expectancy

This bar chart shows you the top 10 countries with highest life expactancy. You can also select a continent that you intesested in to see what's the top 10 countries with highest life expactancy in that continent.

World population distribution by continent

This pie chart shows how are the population distributed in the world by continent, from the chart, we could tell that Asia has more than 50% of the population，And Oceania has Oceania has the smallest population in the world，which is only 0.51%, it makes Oceania hard to be seen in the pie chart

Bubble plot for urbanization rate vs GDP per capita

This plot consists of huge amount of information, every bubble represents for a country, the country with greater population with have a bigger size. Different color represents for the countries in different continent. Also, From this graph, we could tell that GDP per capita and urbanization rate are positively correlated.
